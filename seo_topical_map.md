# Clearcycle IT - SEO Topical Map & Keyword Strategy

This document maps the semantic structure, search intent, and keyword targets for the Clearcycle IT website. It is designed to build search engine authority (E-E-A-T) and drive organic lead generation for B2B IT asset disposal and recycling services.

---

## 1. Target Audience & Search Intent
Our target audience consists of B2B decision-makers across the UK, including:
* **Corporate IT Managers & CTOs**: Focused on logistics, hardware lifecycle value, and decommissioning complexity.
* **Compliance & Risk Officers**: Focused on GDPR audits, data sanitisation certification, and environment licensing.
* **School Business Managers (SBMs) & University ICT Leads**: Focused on budget limits, DBS-cleared staff collections, and academic holiday schedules.

## 6. Optimization Best Practices
* **Header Tags Hierarchy**: Ensure only one `H1` tag per page, followed by `H2` for main headings and `H3` for subheads.
* **Alt Text on Images**: Use descriptive alt tags on all photos (e.g. `alt="DBS-checked collection team loading secure IT assets into transport van"` instead of generic `alt="recycling logo"`).
* **Metadata Schema**: Ensure the dynamic JSON-LD schemas remain aligned with the local search pages.
