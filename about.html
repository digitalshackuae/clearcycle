About Clearcycle IT

We are a professional, licensed IT asset disposal (ITAD) provider built to serve UK organisations, corporate businesses, schools, and local authorities.

OUR PROFILE

A Secure Partner for Corporate Technology Disposal

Clearcycle IT was founded to solve a primary challenge for UK IT managers: how to dispose of retired computers, laptops, and servers safely while maintaining strict data compliance and environmental responsibility.

We operate a dedicated transport fleet and processing facility, ensuring retired assets are tracked throughout the decommissioning lifecycle. We provide legally binding documentation for your records, protecting your organisation from data breaches and regulatory fines.

OUR VALUES

The Principles Driving Our Operations

We manage your computer disposal through strict security, total compliance, and clean environmental practices.

1. Security First

Data security is our primary focus. We treat all retired storage media as sensitive. Whether overwritten via professional sanitisation software or shredded physically, data is rendered fully unrecoverable before materials are recycled.

2. Compliance Guaranteed

Disposal laws are strict. We manage all paperwork - from waste transfer sheets for WEEE compliance to serialization audit trails for data destruction - providing full legal proof of duty of care compliance.

3. Zero Landfill Policy

Obsolete hardware is processed responsibly. We rebuild functional devices to serve in schools or local communities. Damaged hardware is dismantled inside the UK for material recycling.
